About Our Farm

Ethical care • Fresh milk • Healthy animals

We are a family-run dairy farm dedicated to ethical animal care and high-quality milk. Our herd includes cows, buffalo, and goats — all raised on clean feed, fresh water, and with regular veterinary checks.

  • Clean sheds and regular grazing
  • Fodder grown with minimal pesticides
  • Traceable milk collection and chilling
